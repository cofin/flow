# Challenge

Prevents reflexive agreement by forcing structured critical reassessment. References the `perspectives` skill for its critical thinking framework.

## Overview

When a claim, assumption, or proposal needs evaluation, this skill applies structured reassessment: check accuracy, verify completeness, evaluate reasoning quality, investigate when possible, and deliver an honest assessment. No hedging, no meta-commentary — just direct analysis.

## Usage Patterns

- "Challenge this: we should rewrite the auth system in Rust"
- "Are you sure that's the right approach?"
- "I don't think that's correct"
- "Sanity check this assumption"
- "Is this actually a good idea or are you just agreeing with me?"

## How It Works

Follow the strategy in `references/challenge-strategy.md`:

1. **Identify the claim** — extract the core assertion
2. **Apply CRITICAL REASSESSMENT** — check accuracy, completeness, reasoning (from `perspectives/references/critical-thinking.md`)
3. **Investigate** — read code, check docs, verify before responding
4. **Deliver honest assessment** — flaws or confirmation, with specifics
5. **No meta-commentary** — just present the analysis directly
